Bayes Theorem $$P(A|B) = \frac{P(B|A)P(A)}{P(B)}$$

Shannon Entropy $$H(x) = - \sum_{i=1}^{n} p_i log_b(p_i)$$

Priors $$P(class_i) = \frac{\# docs \, of \, class_i}{total \, \# of \, docs}$$

Likelihood $$P(word_i|class_i) = \frac{\# of \, word_i \, in \, class_i}{total \, \# of \, words \, in \, class_i}$$
